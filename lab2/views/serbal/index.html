Jebel Serbal

Sinai Wonder

Jebel Serbal is one of the Sinai's most iconic mountains. A towering mass of rugged peaks, pinnacles and deep, shadowy ravines, it stands in the territory of a Bedouin tribe called the Garasha and is the northernmost of the Seven Summits. Jebel Serbal has long had a sacred significance in the Sinai. Some say its name is derived from the ancient God Baal
